Garlic Confit Recipe

Golden garlic confit in olive oil

Garlic confit is an extremely easy and delicious condiment that's pretty universal to use.

While the cook time may look unappealing for some, it's definitely worth the try.

Ingredients:
  • As much garlic as you want, peeled
  • Olive oil, enough to cover all of your garlic
  • Choice of herbs and spices to taste (basil, peppercorn, thyme, pepper flakes, etc.)
Steps:
  1. Preheat oven to 250 degrees.
  2. Peel garlic and put them into an oven-safe dish.
  3. Add spices and herbs (optional).
  4. Put enough olive oil to cover the garlic completely.
  5. Bake for 2 hours. Once done, transfer to a jar and put into the fridge.
